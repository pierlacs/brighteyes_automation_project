## 1. SCOPE OF CONTRACT

The Ospedale Maggiore Policlinico di Milano issues this public tender for the supply, installation, and maintenance of ophthalmic diagnostic equipment for the renewal of the ophthalmology department's diagnostic platform.

## 2. LOTS

### Lot 1 — Non-mydriatic Retinal Cameras
- Quantity: 3 units
- Minimum resolution: 12 megapixels
- Field of capture: minimum 45°, ideally 60°
- Automatic capture with pupil detection
- DICOM export mandatory
- Compatibility with the existing hospital information system (HIS): Dedalus ORBIS

### Lot 2 — Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Devices
- Quantity: 2 units
- Spectral-domain OCT with scan speed ≥ 80,000 A-scans/second
- Integrated anterior segment module preferred
- Automatic retinal layer analysis
- Integrated normative database
- DICOM export mandatory

### Lot 3 — Automatic Refractometers
- Quantity: 4 units
- Objective refraction measurement
- Integrated keratometer
- Measurement time < 5 seconds
- User interface in Italian

## 3. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Eligibility Criteria
- CE certification (CE medical marking, class IIa minimum)
- Minimum 3 years of experience in supplying ophthalmic equipment
- Minimum annual revenue: €500,000
- References: at least 3 similar installations in public hospitals in Europe within the last 5 years
- On-site maintenance capability within 48 hours

### 3.2 Required Documents
- Company registration certificate (less than 3 months old)
- Professional liability insurance certificate
- CE certificates for each proposed device
- Detailed client references
- Proposed maintenance plan
- Technical staff training certificates

## 4. AWARD CRITERIA

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Technical value of the offer | 50% |
| Price | 30% |
| Delivery and installation timeline | 10% |
| After-sales service quality | 10% |

### Technical value breakdown (50%):
- Performance and technical specifications: 20%
- Ergonomics and ease of use: 10%
- Interoperability and HIS integration: 10%
- Proposed training program: 5%
- Innovation and advanced features (embedded AI, telemedicine): 5%

## 5. FINANCIAL CONDITIONS

- Estimated total budget: €350,000 to €500,000 (excl. VAT)
- Payment terms: 30% on order, 50% on delivery, 20% on final acceptance
- Minimum warranty: 2 years parts and labor
- Optional maintenance contract for 3 additional years

## 6. DELIVERY TIMELINE

- Delivery: maximum 12 weeks after contract notification
- Installation and commissioning: maximum 4 weeks after delivery
- Staff training: to be scheduled within 2 weeks following installation

## 7. LATE DELIVERY PENALTIES

- 0.5% of lot value per calendar day of delay
- Capped at 10% of total contract value

## 8. VARIANTS

Variants are permitted, particularly for:
- Solutions integrating artificial intelligence for automated screening
- Telemedicine ophthalmology solutions
- Multi-lot packages with volume discounts
